About me

I am a full stacked software developer from Hong Kong and based in London, UK. I enjoy writing codes and building side projets in my own time among other hobbies. It means a lot to me that I get to enjoy what we are doing at work, be passionate about learning new skills and improving ourselves, but most importantly be excited and proud of our products. This is how we can get off our bed every morning and start our day with energy and dedication. That is probably why I decided to work in the TV media industry and later the entertainment industry. Yes there are challenges and difficult times just like many other things in life, but on the weekends lying on my favourite yoga mat, I do feel satisfied and grateful as I know I have learnt a few things and have spent my time to work on something meaningful. Later at night there is no better sleep than that.

On a few occasions I was forunate enough to have a the opportunities to travel to Singapore and Spain for work. Not only have I picked up Angular 2 and learnt a lot more about the business operation on the other side of the company, I have also made a few great friends in the trip and have some amazing experiences with different cultures, which I guess I really can't ask for more and makes my role as a software developer a lot more meaningful than just another day job.

Apart from coding and other technical stuff, in my spare time I like to play music, mostly on piano and drums. Sometimes I get to record a few cover songs with friends and share with everyone on our Youtube channel.
